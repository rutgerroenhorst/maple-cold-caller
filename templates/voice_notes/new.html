How voice note screening works

  1. 1. Paste the voice note URL (direct audio link) or skip and paste the transcript manually later.
  2. 2. The system downloads the audio and transcribes it via Whisper (OpenAI).
  3. 3. Claude scores 8 dimensions and gives an A/B/Nurture/Reject verdict.
  4. 4. You review and approve or reject. An action task is created automatically.

⚠ WhatsApp and Telegram voice notes are not directly downloadable — save to your device first and upload to a public link (e.g. Google Drive "anyone with link"), or paste the transcript manually.
